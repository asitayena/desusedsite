Tiffany Blackmon Career

Tiffany Blackmon NFL Network

Tiffany joined NFL Media as a reporter and Atlanta-based correspondent for NFL Network in 2015. She contributes field reports for NFL Network’s shows such as NFL Total Access, Around the NFL, Path to the Draft, NFL GameDay Morning, and more. Prior to joining NFL Media, Tiffany worked as an anchor and reporter for Comcast SportsNet Houston.

Her responsibilities at Comcast SportsNet Houston included covering the Houston Rockets and co-hosting their weekly All-Access show, as well as covering the Houston Texans, the University of Texas, Texas A&M, and Baylor. Tiffany also served as the host of SportsNet Reports.

Prior to working at Comcast SportsNet Houston, worked at the NBC affiliate KFOR-TV in Oklahoma City where she covered the Oklahoma City Thunder as well as football at the University of Oklahoma and Oklahoma State.

Tiffany’s previous experience also includes serving as Sports Director at KXXV-TV in Waco, Texas, and Weekend Sports anchor at KPLC-TV in Lake Charles, Louisiana.
