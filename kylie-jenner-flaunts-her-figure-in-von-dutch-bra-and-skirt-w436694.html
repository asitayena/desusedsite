Kylie Jenner is bringing Von Dutch back! She may have skipped the 2016 MTV VMAs, but the reality TV beauty was still determined to make a style statement with her latest Instagram snap.

Jenner showed off her curves on Sunday, August 28, in a skintight outfit by the brand, that was became famous for its trucker hats.
