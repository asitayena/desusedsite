Sebastian Stan is an actor best known for playing Bucky Barnes/Winter Soldier in the Marvel Cinematic Universe. He made his first appearance as the character in Captain America: The First Avenger.

He is reprising his role in the Disney+ series The Falcon and the Winter Soldier and the Hulu series Pam & Tommy. Before playing the Winter Soldier, he notably played Carter Baizen in Gossip Girl and T.J. Hammond in Political Animals. In addition to Marvel movies, Sebastian has had roles in The Martian, The Bronze, Destroyer, and more. He earned rave reviews for playing Jeff Gillooly in the Tonya Harding biopic I, Tonya with Margot Robbie. Sebastian was born in Romania on August 13, 1982. He became a United States citizen in 2002. Sebastian is one of the most talented actors in Hollywood.
