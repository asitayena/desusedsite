What is Seth Green Most Famous For?

Seth Green has a long and diverse list of credits, but he is probably most famous for his portrayal of Scott Evil, the son of Dr. Evil, in the Austin Powers film series.

He played the role in all three films: Austin Powers: International Man of Mystery (1997), Austin Powers: The Spy Who Shagged Me (1999), and Austin Powers in Goldmember (2002).

He also reprised the role in a cameo appearance in the 2018 film The Lego Batman Movie.

Another role that made him famous is the voice of Chris Griffin, the dim-witted son of Peter and Lois Griffin, on the Fox animated sitcom Family Guy.

He has been voicing the character since the show’s inception in 1999 and has also voiced other characters on the show, such as Neil Goldman, Jake Tucker, and Jeff Fischer.

Seth Green Wife

Seth Green is married to actress, model, and producer Clare Grant.

They met in 2007 at the reopening of their favorite comic book store, Golden Apple Comics, where Green was signing autographs and Grant was taking photos.

They got engaged in 2009 and tied the knot on May 1, 2010, at Skywalker Ranch, the home of filmmaker George Lucas.

The ceremony was officiated by their friend and director Craig Brewer.
