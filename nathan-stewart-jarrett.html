Nathan Stewart-Jarrett is a British actor, theatre artist, and voice actor who is best known for starring as Curtis Donovan in the E4 science fiction comedy-drama series Misfits and as Ian in the Channel 4 thriller drama series Utopia. Some of his other roles include playing Tony in the romantic comedy series Four Weddings and a Funeral, Sam in the dramedy series Generation, Hugh in the black comedy–crime drama film Dom Hemingway, Barrett Hopper in the drama series Famous in Love, Ralph Partridge in the biographical romantic drama film Vita and Virginia, DC Richard Iddon in the crime thriller series Prey, Johnny Edgecombe in the drama The Trial of Christine Keeler, Troy Cartwright in the supernatural slasher film Candyman, Leo Rugazzi in the science fiction show Doctor Who, Georgie in the police procedural series The Bill, Tunde/Luke Whitby in the medical drama series Casualty, and Mr. Kepler in the fantasy action-adventure film The Kid Who Would Be King.
