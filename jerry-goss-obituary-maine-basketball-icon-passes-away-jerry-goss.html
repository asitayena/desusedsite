Jerry was not just an organizer; he was a pillar of support for everyone involved in the basketball tournaments. His dedication and calm demeanor earned him respect and admiration from all who had the privilege of working with him.

His legacy extended beyond his organizational duties; he was a kind and thoughtful individual who touched the lives of many. Whether orchestrating WDEA’s position or ensuring the seamless execution of the tournament, Jerry was a constant, reliable presence.

His commitment to education was mirrored in his enduring marriage to his wife Jean for fifty-two years, and together, they built a loving family with two sons, Jeff and Jon, along with their respective families.

As the Maine High School basketball season kicks off, the community mourns the loss of Jerry Goss, a true leader and friend who will be remembered for his contributions both on and off the court.
