Who is Lori Harvey?

Lori Harvey is the stepdaughter of American comedian, television host, producer, radio personality, actor, and author Steve Harvey. Steve adopted her after marrying Marjorie Harvey. Additionally, she is an American model, former equestrian, and social media influencer. 

Reportedly, Lori Harvey’s biological father is a former drug lord named Donnell Woods. In November 2020, news broke out stating that Lori was dating famous Black Panther actor Michael B. Jordan.

quick facts

Childhood

Lori Harvey was born on January 13, 1997, in Memphis, Tennessee, to Marjorie Harvey and Donnell Woods. She is 26 years old.

The name “Lori” derives from the Latin name “Laurel,” which means “victory and honor.”

Lori was born on January 13, so her sun signs lie under Capricorn. According to her sun sign, she is loyal, disciplined, and intelligent. Furthermore, she is also a highly determined person with a quick mind, which explains her determination to make a place in the modeling world.

Lori Harvey spent most of her life in Atlanta and even completed her high school degree from Atlanta High School in Georgia, after which she attended Florida university.

As a child, she was an avid horseback rider, participated in several competitions, and aspired to participate in the Olympics. However, an accident severely hurt her back, shattering her hopes and dreams. Nevertheless, she became a social media influencer and a model.

Lori Harvey is 5′ 3″ inches (1.6 m) tall and has brown eyes and black-colored hair.

Parents

Even though Lori shares her surname with Steve Harvey, he is not her birth dad. He is her stepdad. Her birth father was a drug lord named Darnell Woods. After he got jailed, her mother, Majorie, met her stepfather, Steve Harvey, and married him decades later.

Steve adopted Lori and her siblings and gave them his last name.

Majorie Harvey

Lori’s mother, Marjorie Harvey, was previously married twice and divorced once before she met Steve Harvey. Her first husband was Jimmy L. Townsend, infamous for being a Memphis drug dealer. Townsend was sentenced to life imprisonment when his illegal business was discovered, leading Lori’s mother to divorce him.

After divorcing Jimmy L. Townsend, Marjorie moved on and tied the knot to another drug lord, Darnell Woods, in 2000. Through Darnell, Marjorie conceived three children, Lori and her two biological sisters and brother, Morgan and Jason Harvey.

Lori’s biological father was charged with drug charges, ending their relationship.

Marjorie is a fashion blogger who runs a blog known as The Lady Loves Couture. Most of her blogs contain content like fashion tips, women empowerment tips, secrets to the beauty world, and many more. She also owns an online shopping site called ‘Marjorie Harvey handbags’ and ‘Marjorie Harvey’s Closet.’

Darnell Woods

Lori Harvey’s biological father is a drug lord named Darnell Woods. He is the second husband of Majorie Harvey. Unfornutaley, he also got arrested for drug-related cases.

Today, her mother is happily married to a comedian named Steve Harvey. He has legally adopted Lori as well as her other three children.

Steve Harvey

Marjorie met Steve Harvey, met in 1987. During that time, Steve was still married to his first wife, Marcia, while Marjorie was unmarried. After divorcing his first wife, Steve did have a brief relationship with Marjorie, but things did not turn out in their favor, and they separated and went their ways.

However, the couple reunited again in 2005 through Steve’s bodyguard, immediately after the comedian went through another divorce from his second wife named Mary.

The couple tied the knot in 2007 and are still very much in love to date. After tieing the knot, Steve legally adopted and gave his name to all three of Marjorie’s children, including Lorie Harvey.

Relationships

She has a history with several famous faces regarding her list of relationships.

Lori Harvey’s first relationship started in 2016 when she dated Dutch player Memphis Depay. After only a year, the couple was engaged in June 2017 but separated after a year in 2018.

After Memphis Depay, Lori moved on to R&B Singer Trey Songz in the same year. Various media outlets and their fans confirmed their relationship as they were spotted together in numerous events like going to jewelry stores or even partying in clubs.

Lori and Trey ended their relationship as the model removed every picture with Trey from her Instagram.

According to TMZ, in 2019, she was dating famous rapper Diddy’s 26-year-old son Justin. They were spotted together at The Official Big Game Takeover, an event hosted by Justin’s father.

However, in July 2019, rumors started to spread that Lori Harvey was dating Diddy. They parted ways around October 2019.

Lori was also briefly connected to F1 champion Lewis Hamilton at a party over Super Bowl weekend in 2019. The couple was making out during the party, but both party ever confirmed the rumors.

By the end of 2019, dating rumors spread, stating that she was in a relationship with Future. The couple celebrated New Year together, and Future even posted a picture of Lori and captioned it ‘flawless.’

However, their relationship ended in August 2020.

She started dating Michael B Jordan in 2020, an American actor famous for playing Erik Killmonger in Black Panther, Oscar Grant in Fruitvale Station, and boxer Donne Creed in Creed. He is ten years older than Lori.

Lori Harvey career

Lori Harvey’s dream to become a professional horseback rider was shattered after she hurt her back due to an accident. After failing to pursue her first career choice, she moved on to pursue a career in modeling.

Lori is not a traditional model like we usually see in ramps. In 2017, Lori Harvey walked in the Paris and Milan Fashion Weeks for Dolce & Gabbana. She has also endorsed products of brands like Hermès and Chanel.

For one event, she was even featured together in a ramp with Christin Combs (Justin Combs’s brother).

Besides walking for designer brands, Lori promotes them on her Instagram account. She has partnered with brands like Hermes and Chanel to promote their products to her followers.

Lori Harvey launched her own beauty company, SKN by LH, on October 22, 2021. The model-turned-entrepreneur revealed that her skincare routine is full of natural ingredients to help prevent skin issues and rejuvenate all skin types.

SKN by LH includes a Vitamin C Serum, Peptide Eye Complex, Goji Berry Cleanser, White Tea Toner, and Niacinamide Cream.
