Tiny and T.I. Welcome Baby Girl, Their Third Child Together

September 06, 2024
post

Lucky No. 7 has arrived! T.I. and his wife, Tiny, welcomed a tiny bundle of joy to the world on Saturday, March 26, the rapper confirmed via Facebook after word spread on social media of the birth.

“What really pisses me off is that I’m in SouthCarolina & my wife just had our baby in Georgia which is really not something anybody could help & the fact that everybody gets to announce that ’T.I. & Tiny welcomed their baby into the world’ EXCEPT T.I & Tiny,” he wrote in a post on Saturday. “Couldn’t even wait until we properly announced it but #Lucky7 is here!”

News of the pair’s latest addition surfaced after Tiny’s niece posted a video to Instagram in which she said she was on her way to visit her aunt, who just had her baby.

T.I. (né Clifford Harris) and Tiny (real name Tameka Cottle) are already parents to two children together — Clifford “King” Joseph Harris III and Major Philant Harris, and have four other children from previous relationships.

Messiah Ya’Majesty Harris and Domani Uriah Harris are T.I.’s sons from his relationship with Lashon Dixon, and Deyjah Imani Harris is his daughter with singer Ms. Niko. Tiny has a daughter, Zonnique Jailee Pullins, whom she shares with ex Zonnie Zebo Pullins.

The couple, who married in 2010, first shared their baby news via an Instagram video on Christmas Day last year.

“Hope you all had a wonderful Christmas and have a blessed Happy New Year!” Tiny, 40, captioned the video reveal. “Thank you for your new heir to the throne!”

On Saturday, the proud mom shared a selfie of herself with good friend, Real Housewives of Atlanta star Kandi Burruss, with a telling caption.

“My sis @kandiburruss came to visit me on this special day!” she wrote. “I love her 4life!! #Lucky7.”
