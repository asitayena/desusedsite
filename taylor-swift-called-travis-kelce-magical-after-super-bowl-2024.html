Taylor Swift called Travis Kelce 'magical' after Super Bowl 2024

August 17, 2024
post

Taylor Swift got lost in the Super Bowl 2024 glory, especially while watching boyfriend Travis Kelce taking in his third title in the last five seasons.

“You are so magical,” Swift said to Kelce on a hot mic, according to TMZ, while the two embraced on the field after the Chiefs 25-22 victory over the 49ers in Las Vegas.

“I’ve never been so proud in my life,” Swift added. “I can’t believe you. How did you do that? How did you do it?”

The 14-time Grammy winner has been publicly linked with Kelce since the summer of last year and has since made 13 showings at Chiefs games across the season, including all three playoff games leading up to the Super Bowl at Allegiant Stadium.

Kelce, answering Swift, praised her for taking the lengthy trip from Tokyo, where she put on four shows on her Eras Tour, to land in L.A. and then take a separate flight to Las Vegas in order to attend the big game.

“How do you not have jet lag right now?” he said.

“Jet lag is a choice,” Swift responded.

The couple continued the celebration all night long, hopping around to multiple clubs in the city.

Swift even gave an inside look to the afterparty in a TikTok video showing her and Kelce clubbing with her parents, Andrea and Scott Swift.

“Accidentally going clubbing with your parents is something everyone should try at least once in your life,” she wrote in the caption.

In the 17-second clip, Kelce stuck his tongue out and then the camera panned to Scott chugging a beer as Andrea sat next to him, smiling.

“It’s a friends and family party they said,” Swift wrote over the video. “Bring your parents they said.”

Swift had to fly to Australia to resume her tour and did not attend the Chiefs’ Super Bowl parade, which was marred by a shooting afterward that killed one at injured at least 22, including nine children.
