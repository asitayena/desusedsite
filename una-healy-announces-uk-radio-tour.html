Una Healy Announces UK Radio Tour

August 31, 2024
post

Former The Saturdays singer Una Healy is currently on a promotional tour around the UK and Ireland promoting her brilliant debut solo single ‘Stay My Love’, which features the Nashville star Sam Palladio.

The song is a departure from the pop sound Una Healy created while part of the girlband, and she has instead gone back to her country/folk roots.

The 35-year-old rising star of British country music also released a behind-the-scenes video of the official music video shoot for the single which was filmed in Nashville last year.

And now Una Healy who has just returned from a radio tour of Ireland has announced the dates of her UK radio tour. She will be visiting stations in Cardiff, Swansea, Newcastle and many more.

Dates and times for the tour have not been announced although it has been confirmed that Una will be in Wales from tomorrow morning.

‘Stay My Love‘ is the first single to be released from Una Healy’s upcoming debut album ‘The Waiting Game‘ which drops on the 10th of February.
