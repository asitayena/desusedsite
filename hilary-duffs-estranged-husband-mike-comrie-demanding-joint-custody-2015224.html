Hilary Duff’s estranged husband Mike Comrie is seeking joint custody of their 3-year-old son Luca, TMZ reports. The move comes after Duff had filed for primary custody of their son in her February filing.

According to TMZ, the only point of contention for the couple — who are agreeing to their prenup to sort out their assets — is their little guy. 

As previously reported, the Younger star wants primary custody of Luca because she’s concerned about Comrie’s partying. Just one day before she filed for divorce this past February, the Lizzie Mcguire alum’s ex reportedly drunkenly hit on a waitress at Mastro’s in Beverly Hills, throwing money at her and asking her for sex.
